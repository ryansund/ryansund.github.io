Tips and Tricks!

  • Make sure to double check your coding so you prevent mistakes. If you don't check it, you will have to go back and troubleshoot it.
  • Use your resources. W3Schools and PLTW can help give you many great ideas.
  • Let your peers help you. There are always going to be people smarter than you, so use your pair programmers to your advantage.
  • Always trouble shoot. Often times you can learn more about coding from trouble shooting, than from coding itself.
